Taco Dinner

Description

These tacos remind me of my great aunt Juniper who fought in all three world wars. She didn't have much time to spend with us on account of all her fellow time travelers who were trying to kill her for talking about WW3. So we made these tacos in a quick hurry whenever she came over. I remember munching on these while asking how she found that time-travel ring she always wore. Apparantly it was her late husband's before he was asssassinated by the time traveler's secret society. Oh, and I guess these tacos taste alright.

    Ingredients:
  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 1 Birds EyeÂ® Recipe Ready Southwest Blend (12 ounces)
  • 1 box (11.04) ounces of taco dinner kit
  • 1/4 cup of water
  • Sour cream
  • Shredded lettuce
    Steps:
  1. Brown ground beef in large nonstick skillet; drain fat.
  2. Add Recipe Ready Southwest Blend, taco seasoning and water. Cook over medium-high heat, stirring occasionally, 5 minutes or until vegetables are tender.
  3. Serve in tortillas and taco shells topped with taco sauce, shredded lettuce and sour cream.
